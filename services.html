Services in Mombasa County

Mombasa county has different serivces which enable it to be economic
growing region

Some of the social section carried out include the following

  1. education
  2. health
  3. Tourism
  4. industrial

Budget allocation

During each finacial year the govermnet allocate the funds to
improve the services in each section to enhance development.

The allocation is a follows.

  1. education = 35%
  2. health = 40%
  3. Tourism = 10%
  4. industrial = 15%
